Company profile

Built to supply, serve, and support Zambia's growing industries.

Tingon Enterprises Limited is a Zambian company providing general supply, construction, technical, engineering, food, farming, and operational support services from Lusaka.

Background

A practical partner for broad procurement needs.

The company was established by indigenous Zambians to close market gaps by offering dependable services across livestock, farming, construction, food stuffs, electronics, engineering, general supply, and general dealing.

Tingon Enterprises Limited works to build lasting relationships by exceeding client expectations, earning trust through performance, and maintaining a responsive approach from enquiry through delivery.
