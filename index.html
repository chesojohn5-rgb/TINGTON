Company profile

A truly Zambian company built for dependable supply.

Tingon Enterprises Limited was established by indigenous Zambians to help close market gaps through wide-ranging supply, construction, technical, and general dealing services.

The company focuses on long-term customer relationships, quality delivery, cost efficiency, timely service, and a practical understanding of client needs.
